Pizza Fry Poppers:
Lunch
2023-11-12
Ingredients:

-1 package of pre-made pizza dough

-1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese

-1/4 cup of diced pepperoni

-1/2 cup of cooked French fries

-1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

-1 egg

-1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder

-1/4 teaspoon of dried oregano

-Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.

2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out pizza dough into a 12-inch round.

3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, and French fries over the pizza dough, leaving a 1-inch border.

4. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the toppings.

5. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper.

6. Brush the egg mixture over the edges of the pizza dough.

7. Fold the edges of the pizza dough up and over the toppings, pinching the edges to seal.

8. Place the pizza on a greased baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.

9. Let cool for 5 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
