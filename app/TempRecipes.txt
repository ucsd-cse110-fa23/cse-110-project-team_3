Egg and Bacon Breakfast Burrito
Breakfast
2023-11-12
Ingredients:

- 4 large eggs
- 4 slices of bacon
- 2 tbsp. of butter
- 1/4 cup of shredded cheese
- 1/4 cup of diced bell peppers
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 4 large flour tortillas

Instructions:

1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the bacon until it's crispy. Remove it from the skillet and set aside.

2. In the same skillet, add the butter and bell peppers. Cook until the peppers are tender.

3. Crack the eggs into the skillet and cook until they are firm.

4. Add the bacon and cheese to the eggs and stir until the cheese is melted.

5. Divide the egg and bacon mixture into four equal parts and spoon it into the center of each tortilla.

6. Sprinkle salt and pepper over the egg and bacon mixture.

7. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling and roll it up.

8. Serve warm with your favorite accompaniments. Enjoy!
